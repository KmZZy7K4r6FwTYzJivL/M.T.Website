2025 · Moving Image

cry.sis

1 second video work for the One-Off Moving Image Festival 2024, centred around the theme "I cry and I cry".

The video was screened from November 15th to 24th 2024 at ten street venues in Copenhagen, Frederiksberg, Christianshavn, Helsingør and Køge.
